PEOPLE PORTAL

Don't be yourself.

Be everyone else.

Have you ever wished you had someone else’s digital identity?

People Portal is an iPhone app that simulates the experience of using Twitter as if you’re signed in from another user account of your choice.

  • Choose any of the 600+ million Twitter users.

  • See the same timeline they see.

  • Receive the same notifications they receive.

Live in the future of frictionless identity adoption, today.



People Portal is an artwork by Anastasis Germanidis.

Its source code is freely available on Github.
